Roasted Pepper

Thick red peppers are great for roasting. They're great raw too but roasting brings out a new flavor (and smell) which makes the pepper usable in different contexts.

Buy thick red peppers
Put the oven rack in the middle
Preheat to 400
Cut long slits on 3 different sides
Arrange on baking sheet without crowding
Bake 20-30 min (depends how many and how thick)
They should be deflated and blackened
Flip and bake again for the same duration
Let them cool in a closed container
Peel
