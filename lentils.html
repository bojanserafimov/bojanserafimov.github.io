Lentils

Macedonian recipe for green lentils in liquid form.

> Cook aromatics. Same as the tomato sauce recipe.

> Add liquids. A small can of tomatoes (see tomato sauce recipe for recommendation) and some water (see package for quantity).

> Add lentils

> Season. > Granulated chicken bouillon and dried vegetables spice

> Simmer covered for 1 hour

> Add half a spoon of flour. Maybe mix it in warm water first
