Tomato Sauce

Shopping
salt + pepper
extra virgin olive oil
Peeled canned tomatoes, san marzano
tomato paste
1 onion
4 cloves garlic or more
maybe carrot, celery
maybe thyme, basil
Dishes
Large pot
Wooden spoon
Jars
Cook aromatics
Add onion and thick layer of oil
Cook 10-15 min until soft. Don't smoke the oil
Add thin diced garlic, cook until it smells good (less than minute)
Cook tomatoes
Add tomatoes
Add tomato paste
Heat until the sauce reduces
Seasoning, herbs, testing
Maybe add thyme, thin sliced basil, salt, pepper
If too sour add sugar or fat, or keep cooking on low (up to 1h is fine)
Good combos
Pasta
Grilled cheese
Storage options
Jar while it's hot
Fridge for 2-3 days
Freeze forever
