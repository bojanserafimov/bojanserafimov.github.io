Tomato Sauce

  1. Add onion to pan. Almost submerge in olive oil
  2. Cook 10-15 min until soft. Don't smoke the oil
  3. Add thin diced garlic, cook until it smells good (less than minute)
  4. Add tomatos
  5. Add tomato paste
  6. Heat until the sauce reduces
  7. Maybe add thyme, thin sliced basil, salt, pepper
  8. If too sour add sugar or fat, or keep cooking on low (up to 1h is fine)
