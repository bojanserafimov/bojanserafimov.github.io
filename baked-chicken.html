Baked Chicken

> Get dark chicken meat. Dark meat is easier to use because it dries out less. So you can overcook it 10 min and it's still great. I used boneless skinless thighs.
> Put it in stone baking dish. Stone is easier to wash than metal.
> Pour Italian dressing
> Flip and add more
> Bake on 400 uncovered
> Take it out when it looks browned
