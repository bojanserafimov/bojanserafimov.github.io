Hummus

Shop
Dried (not canned!) chickpeas
Tahini
Garlic
Extra virgin olive oil
Lemon
Salt
Soak overnight
Cook chickpeas
Change the water
Add 1 tsp baking soda
Bring to boil
Simmer on low for 40 min (no lid)
Roast garlic
Peel 3-4 heads of garlic
Put in saucepan
Cover with oil
Cook on low for 40 min (no lid)
Garlic should look nutty and smell good
Don't dump the oil
Process chickpeas
Rinse
Peel
Mash
Mix
Add chickpeas
Add 1/4 cup tahini (mix the jar!)
Squeeze one lemon (no seeds!)
Add 1/4-1 cups of the garlic
Add 1/3 cup of the garlic olive oil
Add salt
