About The Cookbook

I wanted to learn how to cook stuff beyond just boiled eggs. So I googled "beginner recipes" and only found recipes that:
- take hours to make
- require washing 5 dishes
- use special ingredients
- skip the basics of why things work
- induce anxiety
- are unreadable because of ads
- are paywalled
- contain irrelevant stories and fluff
- are hard to sight-read while cooking

A bit much for beginners, wouldn't you agree? I also found it suspicious that no list of "beginner recipes" starts with "here's how to boil eggs". So I decided to document my learning process and maybe come back to it later and write a comperhensive "complete cooking guide for millenials". The goal is not to give you the trendiest recipes so that you can try impress your father-in-law, but to help you:
- take ownership of the kitchen
- build the habit of cooking every day
- understand the basic ingredients, techniques, tools
- focus on recipes that are worth the effort
- have an alternative to ordering disappointing food
- write your own recipes

But first I need to learn this stuff myself.
