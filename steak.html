Steak

Shopping
Ribeye, 1 lb for 1-2 people
fresh rosemary
4 cloves garlic
coarse salt
pepper + grinder
Thaw if frozen
Season
Cover in salt and pepper
Leave for 30 min (or longer) in fridge
Cook
Heat cast iron
Melt butter 1/3 stick
Cook steak and garlic for 3 min
Flip, cook 3 min. Don't move it
During last 30 sec add rosemary
Rest
Remove from pan
Pour the juice over it
Let it rest for 5 min
