Egg Fried Rice (content warning: sacrilege)

I followed another white boy's recipe and turned my boiled rice leftovers into a full delicious meal in 10 minutes. there's a lot to improve here, but it's good enough to make again.

Get pan that handles heat, maybe a wok? I don't know if it would be useful without the special wok stovetop and special burner. I used a cast iron skillet. It was fine because it fit all my rice and I didn't spill much while stirring, but it's not ideal since it takes long to respond to heat.
Get soy sauce
Chop some green onion
Whisk some eggs in a bowl
Heat a good amount of high-temperature oil (veg or canola)
Cook scrambled eggs until 70% done
Add cold boiled rice. People suggest adding sesame oil + normal oil to the rice before cooking, to prevent sticing. I haven't tried that yet.
Break up the chunks and mix everything. A spatula might work but I've also seen people use the bottom of a ladle for this.
Cook and mix until all the rice grains break up
Turn the heat to high
Add a drop of soy sauce (it should sizzle)
Mix everything and stir the rice
Things should start smelling good
When a good number of grains are toasted, turn off the heat
Add green onions
Season and stir
