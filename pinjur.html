Pinjur

Pinjur (j as in "Jurry") is a Macedonian side dish. If your peppers or tomatoes are close to going bad make this and put it in the fridge. This is not the traditional recipe, I just YOLO-ed it.

Roast and peel some peppers
Add peeled tomatoes
Add raw garlic
Add salt, pepper, olive oil
Blend in blender
Eat with toasted fresh bread
