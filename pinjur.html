Пинџур (Pinjur)

Pinjur (rhymes with "injure") is a Macedonian side dish. If your peppers or tomatoes are close to going bad make this and put it in the fridge.

Get blender
Add peeled tomatoes
Add raw garlic
Add salt, pepper, olive oil, vinegar
Blend
Drain the liquid
Alternatively, simmer to reduce
Roast and peel peppers >>>
Either dice or blend
Mix everything
Eat with toasted fresh bread
Store in fridge
