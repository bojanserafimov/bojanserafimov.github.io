Alfredo Sauce

Easy white pasta sauce. Works well on rice too.

> Get saucepan
> Add 1 stick of butter
> Add 1 cup of heavy cream
> Heat on low until butter melts
> Add 1 cup shredded parmesan. Any kind of parmesan works, but shredding a block yourself might work better than using the pre-shredded kind. The latter has stuff in it to prevent it from sticking to itself, so it will retain some grainy texture, which you might not like.
